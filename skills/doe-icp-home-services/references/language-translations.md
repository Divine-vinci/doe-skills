# Language Translation Guide

Mike does not speak "web design." Every technical concept must be translated into plain English that ties to business impact (missed calls, lost customers, money left on the table).

## The Translation Table

| Technical Term | Say This Instead |
|---------------|-----------------|
| PageSpeed score of 28 | "Your site takes 9 seconds to load — most people leave after 3" |
| Not mobile responsive | "Your site is hard to read on a phone — that's where 80% of your customers are looking" |
| No SSL certificate | "Your site shows a 'Not Secure' warning — customers see that and leave" |
| No CTA | "There's no easy way for someone to call you from your website" |
| High bounce rate | "People are finding your site and leaving without calling" |
| Low conversion rate | "Your website isn't turning visitors into phone calls" |
| SEO | "Showing up when people Google 'plumber near me'" |
| Responsive design | "Works perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers" |
| Fast load time | "Opens instantly when someone clicks" |
| Click-to-call | "One tap and they're calling you" |
| Google Business Profile | "Your Google listing — the one that shows up on Google Maps" |
| Meta tags | "The preview that shows up when your site appears on Google" |
| Schema markup | "The stars and phone number that show next to your Google listing" |
| Above the fold | "What someone sees BEFORE they scroll" |
| Lead magnet | "A free thing that gets people to give you their info" |
| Sales funnel | "The steps that turn a visitor into a phone call" |
| A/B testing | "Trying two versions to see which gets more calls" |
| Content marketing | "Writing useful stuff that helps customers find you" |
| Google Analytics | "A dashboard that shows who's visiting your site" |
| Heatmap | "A picture showing where people click on your site" |

## The Business Impact Framing

Always end with WHAT IT COSTS HIM:

- Slow site → "That's half your Google Ads traffic bouncing before they see your phone number."
- No mobile → "80% of your potential customers are on phones — you're losing most of them."
- No reviews shown → "Your 4.8 stars are invisible. Customers don't trust you."
- No click-to-call → "People have to manually type your number. Most won't."
- Old design → "Your site looks like it's from 2010 — customers wonder if you're still in business."

## Converting Audit Findings to Sales Briefs

**Bad (technical)**: "The site has a PageSpeed mobile score of 27, no viewport meta tag, and lacks schema.org/LocalBusiness markup."

**Good (Mike's language)**: "Your site loads in 9 seconds on phones. Most people give up after 3. Your 4.8 Google rating isn't showing up in search results because your site isn't set up to tell Google who you are."

**Bad**: "The hero section has no call-to-action above the fold, and the CTA below the fold is a mailto: link."

**Good**: "There's no phone number at the top of your page. People have to scroll to find it — or email you. Plumbers don't get jobs from email."
